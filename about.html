    Power-House behind the Solar Trekker

    In the diligent, hard-working team behind the production of the solar-powered car "Solar Trekker", we focus on providing reliable cars that run on clean, renewable energy and reduce the utilization of fossil fuels. We hope to enhance air quality by reducing CO2 emissions, which are emitted in bulk quantities from conventional gasoline-powered vehicles. Look around our website and if you have any comments or questions, please feel free to contact us. We hope to see you again!

    Solar Trekker Is A Nonprofit Site and Isn't Used for Commercial Purposes.
